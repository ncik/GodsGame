Usurper*

Chosen:
If the Hard Worker is not chosen, gain VP instead of the King
Counts as a blue card when calculating the King's VP

Grunt

Chosen:
If the King dies, gain 4 VP
Not chosen:
Gain 2 VP

Thief*

Chosen:
Gain bonus 1 VP
Steal 1 VP from everyone chosen and 1 VP from the King
Not Chosen:
Steal 1 VP from one player who wasn't chosen but gained VP this turn

The King*

If completing the quest alone:
Gain 1 VP
If completing the quest with 1 blue card:
Gain 2 VP
If completing the quest with 2 blue cards:
Gain 4 VP
If completing the quest with 3 blue cards:
Gain 6 VP
Each red card chosen cancels out a blue card
If you choose the Heir, the Hand of the King or the Peasant, gain 1 bonus VP 
The Heir, the Hand of the King and the Peasant each 

The Heir

Chosen:
Gain 2 VP
Gain 1 bonus VP if you become King at the end of the round
The King gains 1VP
If the King dies, you become the new King

Cleric

Chosen:
Gain 1 VP
Become King after the round
Always:
Gain 1 VP per blue card chosen

Hand of the King

Chosen:
Gain 3 VP
Gain 1 bonus VP if the Assassin is also chosen
The King gains 1 VP

Hard Worker*

Chosen:
Split VP gained with the King this round (except bonus VP)
Prevents the Usurper from gaining VP instead of the King
Become the new King at the end of the round

Troubadour

Chosen:
Transform all red cards chosen to blue cards when calculating the King's points this round
Gain 3 VP for each card transformed

Peasant*

Chosen:
The King gains 1 VP
Not Chosen:
Gain 2 VP
Always:
If the King dies, gain 1 bonus VP

Assassin

Chosen:
If the Hand of the King is also chosen, you die immediately
If the Hand of the King is not chosen, you kill the King and gain 3 VP
Become the King at the end of the round if the Heir is not chosen

Poison Master

Chosen:
You kill the King and gain 3 VP
Become the King at the end of the round if the Heir is not chosen

Archbishop

Chosen:
Gain 1VP
Become the King at the end of the round
Always:
Gain 1VP per red card chosen
