The King

If you choose 1 blue card, gain 2 VP.
If you choose 2 blue cards, gain 4 VP.
If you choose 3 blue cards, gain 6 VP.

Each red card chosen cancels out a blue card.
If you choose an equal number of blue and red cards, gain 1 VP.

Choosing the following cards grants you 1 bonus VP each:
    The Prince, the Royal Guard and the Peasant.
    

Manipulator

IF CHOSEN WITHOUT THE DIPLOMAT
  Gain VP instead of the King this round (except bonus VP).

ALWAYS
  Counts as a blue card when calculating the King's VP.

Grunt

IF CHOSEN AND THE KING DIES
  Gain 4 VP.

IF NOT CHOSEN
  Gain 2 VP.

Thief

IF CHOSEN
  Gain 1 bonus VP.
  Steal 1 VP from the King.

IF CHOSEN AND NOT ALONE
  Steal 1 VP from every other chosen player.

IF NOT CHOSEN
  Steal 1 VP from one player who wasn't chosen but gained VP this turn.

The Prince

IF CHOSEN
  Gain 2 VP.
  The King gains 1 VP.

IF CHOSEN AND THE KING DIES
  Gain 2 bonus VP.
  Become the King at the end of the round.

Duke

IF CHOSEN
  Gain 1 VP.
  Become the King at the end of the round.

ALWAYS
 Gain 1 VP per blue card chosen.

Royal Guard

IF CHOSEN
  Gain 3 VP.
  The King gains 1 VP.

IF CHOSEN WITH THE ASSASSIN
  Gain 1 bonus VP.
  The Assassin dies.

Diplomat

IF CHOSEN
  Split VP gained with the King this round (except bonus VP).
  Become the King at the end of the round.

IF CHOSEN WITH THE MANIPULATOR
  Prevents the Manipulator from gaining VP instead of the King.
  Gain 1 VP.

Jester

IF CHOSEN
  If possible, you must switch cards with any other chosen player before scoring at the end of the round.
  Gain 2 VP if you have this card at the end of the round.

Peasant

IF CHOSEN
  The King gains 1 VP.

IF NOT CHOSEN
  Gain 2 VP.

ALWAYS
  If the King dies, gain 1 bonus VP.

Assassin

IF CHOSEN WITH THE ROYAL GUARD
  You die and score no VP this round.

IF CHOSEN WITHOUT THE ROYAL GUARD
  You kill the King.
  Gain 3 VP.

IF CHOSEN WITHOUT THE PRINCE AND THE KING DIES
  Become the King at the end of the round.

Alchemist

IF CHOSEN
  You kill the King.
  Gain 3 VP.

IF CHOSEN ALONE
  Gain 1 bonus VP.

Archbishop

IF CHOSEN
  Gain 1 VP.
  Become the King at the end of the round.

ALWAYS
  Gain 1 VP per red card chosen.
